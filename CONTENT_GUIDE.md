# Freedom Clock content guide

## Canonical definition

Freedom time is the number of years and months your savings, Bitcoin, and other assets could support your current monthly spending without new earned income. It answers the plain question: if I stopped earning tomorrow, how long could I keep living the life I want?

Use this definition verbatim on the homepage, `/freedom-time/`, schema descriptions for the concept, README copy, talks, and posts.

## Page roles

- `/freedom-time/` defines the term and owns the query "what is freedom time".
- `/use-freedom-time/` explains what to do with the number after someone has calculated it.
